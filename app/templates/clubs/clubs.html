Clubs and Societies

Summary

Alliance High School is a center of building an all rounded student. Each student is required to join at least two clubs, one being religious. There are several strong clubs which are student run. Each club can organize events and invite several speakers who help in the general well being of the club. Club officials include:

  • Chairman
  • Vice Chairman
  • Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Organizing Secretary
  • Activities Co-ordinator
  • Administrator
  • Form(1, 2, 3) Representatives
  • Prayer Co-ordinators
Appointment of senior club officials is overseen by the Deputy Principal Administration. Club chairmen who aren't appointed prefects are appointed as Senior Commoners.

A club is headed by a patron who is approached by the founders of the club. The patron helps in organizing events and is directly answerable to the principal on matters pertaining to the club. The patron can be assisted by other teachers in the club.

Each club has its own account in which all contributions necessary for running the club are kept. The treasurer accounts for any deposits and withdrawals. The patron overseas expenditure of club funds.
