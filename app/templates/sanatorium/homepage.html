Health Care

The Alliance High School is a center of quality health care. Endowed with a sanatorium and a recently built Level 5 ward, the school aims at ensuring the health of students, staff and residents within the school is catered for.

The sanatorium is manned by the school nurse with their assistants if needed. The duties of the nurse include the following but are not limited to:

  • Attending to patients
  • Making prescriptions for patients
  • Authorizing and supervising students going to hospital
  • Issuing exemption cheats
  • Restocking the sanatorium medical stock
  • Reporting on the health condition of the school population
  • Carrying out house inspections and compiling reports
  • Following up on students with doctor appointments
  • Following up on in-patients
  • Ensuring discipline is maintained in the sanatorium

The school has a strong policy towards storage of medicine. All medicinal substances upon reporting to school are to be taken to the sanatorium. Having any medicine in the house area is a punishable offense.

All patients headed to the hospital use the school minibus. The school's preferred hospitals include:

  • St. Teresa Kikuyu Nursing and Maternity Home
  • PCEA Kikuyu Hospital
